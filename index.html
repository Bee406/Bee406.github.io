About me

I am a freelance web designer with a particular focus on the user experience. My background in hospitality and marketing have given me a unique perspective on the many phases of the user interaction. I am driven by my passion for finding multiple solutions to any given problem - I've found there’s rarely a single right answer - and continue to expand on my professional experience as well as my academic foundations in psychology and media studies.

I’m always looking for a new puzzle to figure out. Please feel free to reach out at kimclary@mac.com.
